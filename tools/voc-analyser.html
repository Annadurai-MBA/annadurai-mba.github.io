Multi-source by design

Accepts inputs from all five channels. Minimum two sources required for defensible prioritization. Source attribution preserved in output.

B2B industrial pre-configured

Built-in understanding of multi-stakeholder buying, long cycles, compliance requirements, and cost-of-ownership dynamics. No prompting needed.

Output connects to HoQ

JTBD requirement statements, importance weights 1 to 9, buyer role, and source attribution. Drop directly into House of Quality left column.

Live mode

Analyse your data

Paste your own VoC inputs from up to five internal sources. Add your Gemini API key to run the analysis. Optionally enrich with live market intelligence from the web.

Your data stays in your browser. The API key is used only for this session and is never stored.

This tool is scoped to B2B engineered physical goods: sensors, connectors, PCBs, measurement instruments, pumps, actuators, drives, valves, and sealing systems. It does not replace QFD or House of Quality. It produces the structured customer requirements that feed into the HoQ left column. The QFD session itself still requires human experts from quality, engineering, sales, and business development.
